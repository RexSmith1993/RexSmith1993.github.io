TATA Power - India

I worked for TATA Power, India form 2015 to 2019. During my first two years, I was tasked with the operation and maintenance of thermal and hydro-electric power plants. And the during the latter two, I worked on multiple R&D projects to automate various plant processes.

Lead Engineer - Corporate Digitalization Group, 2017-2019

  • As a Lead Engineer in the Corporate Digitalization Group, I was mainly responsible for implementing Industry 4.0 processes in the Generation, Transmission, and Distribution divisions. I did this by understanding the user's requirements and then tailoring customized engineering solutions to best fit these requirements.
    To know more about the projects I worked on, please click on the titles below.

  • Tools used: Visual Studio Code (C/C++), Ultimaker Cura, Thingsboard, Arduino IDE, MAVLink, QGroundControl

  • CAD Packages used: CATIA, EasyEDA

  • Materials worked on: ABS, Carbon Fibre, Aluminium

Lead Engineer - Operation & Maintenance, 2015-2017

  • As Lead Engineer in the Operations and Maintenance department, I was responsible for the day-to-day operations of the power plant equipment, including 24MWx3 hydro electric generators and 110/220 KV Switchyard. I was also responsible for the preparation of techno-commercial documents like daily and monthly MIS reports. Apart form my regular responsibilities, I also carried out smaller automation projects around the power plant. To see more details about these please click the titles below.

  • Tools/Applications used: Microsoft Office, WINCC, SCADA
